About Us

Founded in 2025, Maphasa Tech Solutions is a small tech startup focused on providing affordable software solutions to local businesses in South Africa. The initial goal is to enhance digital transformation in underserved regions by making technology accessible and functional. Over the years, the company will expand its operations to include a global client base, with a growing portfolio in cloud services, mobile app development, and cybersecurity solutions.
